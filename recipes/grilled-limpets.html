Lapas Grelhadas (Grilled Limpets)

Description

Lapas Grelhadas are a traditional seafood dish from Madeira Island, Portugal. Limpets are grilled in their shells with a simple mixture of garlic, butter, and lemon juice. The flavor is rich, slightly salty, and fresh from the sea. They are usually served as a starter with bread to soak up the delicious sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500g fresh limpets (lapas)
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 50g butter
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh parsley (optional)

Steps

  1. Clean the limpets and place them on a tray with the shell facing down.
  2. Crush the garlic and melt the butter in a pan.
  3. Add the garlic to the butter and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Drizzle the garlic butter over each limpet.
  5. Grill over high heat for 5-7 minutes until cooked.
  6. Remove from heat and add lemon juice on top.
  7. Garnish with parsley and serve immediately with bread.
